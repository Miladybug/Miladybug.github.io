Glacier & Waterton Lakes Bike Tour

It's called Big Sky. Yet you're still amazed by how big it is. You enjoy miles of scenic roads and the sound of your wheels spinning. You ride and talk. You ride and don't talk. You're awestruck by a waterfall's force. You question why you don't live closer to the mountains. You're in two magnificent national parks. And you'll never be the same.

Cycle the Continential Divide

The Great Divide Mountain Bike Route (GDMBR) is the most recognized and important off-pavement cycling route in the United States, if not the world. The route crisscrosses the Continental Divide from north to south starting in Banff, Alberta, Canada and finishing at the US/Mexico border in Antelope Wells, New Mexico.

Yellowstone National Park Bide Tour

A Yellowstone National Park bike tour is a otherworldly experience especially if you like wildlife, geysers and fabulous landscapes. This tour spends three days in the National Park and then 4 days in the lessor know, but still amazing Greater Yellowstone Ecosystem. We consider Day 7’s Bear Tooth Pass to be one of the top three climbs on any of our 30 bike touring itineraries!
Join the local experts at Lizard Head Cycling Guides as we explore the Yellowstone Country together and are challenged by some of North America’s most amazing cycling terrain.
